Homemade Pizza

Make delicious homemade pizza with this easy recipe. Perfect for a family dinner or a casual get-together with friends.

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Pizza dough 1 ball
Tomato sauce 1 cup
Mozzarella cheese 2 cups, shredded
Pepperoni 1 cup, sliced
Olive oil 2 tablespoons
Fresh basil 1/4 cup, chopped
Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce.
  5. Arrange the pepperoni slices evenly over the cheese.
  6. Drizzle the olive oil over the pizza and sprinkle with salt.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  8. Remove from the oven and sprinkle with fresh basil before serving.
