Focaccia Pizza

Make delicious Focaccia pizza with this easy recipe. Perfect for a family dinner or a casual get-together with friends.

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Pizza dough 1 ball
Tomato sauce 1 cup
mozzarella cheese 2 cups, shredded
Pepperoni 1 cup, sliced
Olive oil 2 tablespoons
burrata cheese 1/4 cup, chopped
Salt Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. ollow the Best Pizza Dough or Thin Crust Pizza Dough recipe to prepare the dough. (This takes about 15 minutes to make and 45 minutes to rest.)
  2. Place a pizza stone in the oven and preheat to 500°F. OR preheat your pizza oven (here’s the pizza oven we use).
  3. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce.
  5. Arrange the pepperoni slices evenly over the cheese.
  6. Drizzle the olive oil over the pizza and sprinkle with salt.
  7. Use the pizza peel to pull the pizza out of the oven. Working quickly, spread the burrata chunks evenly over the dough. Place it back in the oven and cook 3 to 5 minutes until the cheese is melted..
  8. Allow the pizza to cool for a minute before adding the basil on top (whole leaves, lightly torn, or thinly sliced). Slice into pieces and serve immediately.
